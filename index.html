About Me

Hello! My name is Ian Castro and I am a graduate student at the UC Berkeley School of Information, studying applied data science. I completed my undergraduate degree also at UC Berkeley, where I worked at the Division of Computing, Data Science, and Society, and majored in Media Studies and Microbial Biology. I'm particularly interested in accessible STEM education, and my background is in teaching and designing introductory computing courses.
